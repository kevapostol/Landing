About Us

Kanvas is a professional networking site for both Software Engineer students and entry level developers. You can make connections, share posts, and job search all in one place. It is true that you can succeed most and quickest by helping others to succeed.​​ We created Kanvas because we wanted to reframe the process of building a career. Climbing up the ladder is great, but it doesn't encompass all the different experiences each person has. It's not only linear. It's more than climbing to the top. Think of your career as a work of art. Each move is synonymous to the increment movements you make to create a masterpiece.
